Register for Local Shop Affliate Id (LSAID)

We'll never share your email with anyone else.

Once registered you will recieve email and phone message with your LSAID. This LSAID is a ten letter key unique to you.

Start Social Media Marketing.

For any media platform all you are doing is sharing the local shop product link. An example link 

https://www.vlocalshop.in/product/QBMVB6B35O
Before sharing the link to whatsapp or facebook or your website, append the lsaid to the product link. Example Below: 
https://www.vlocalshop.in/product/QBMVB6B35O?lsaid={yourlsaid}

We track every order that comes to us based on the LSAID, if there is an order generated from your leads we will feed into the system and you will be notified on your email and phone number.You can also track your lead orders by entering your LSAID here.
