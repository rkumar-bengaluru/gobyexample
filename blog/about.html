More About Us

vlocalshop.in is an authentic online local store. We offer a broad range of brands in a speciality store environment. We provide an online platform for our customers to buy original, genuine products.

We offer a unique pricing model wherein you can see how much other ecommerce players like amazon, flipkart are charging compared to our platform. This unique method will help customer getting the same product at a cheaper price without compromising on the quality of the product being delivered. We can offer at a much cheaper price because we do not charge any commission to our sellers each product, instead we take an upfront yearly fee from our sellers and sellers can directly communicate and deliver products to customers.

vlocalshop.in was launched in 2018 and we started with 1 pin code and as of today we offer our services to 20+ pin codes in Bengaluru.
